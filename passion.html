About Golf

Golf is a precision club-and-ball sport in which players use various clubs to hit balls into a series of holes on a course in as few strokes as possible. It is played on a large outdoor course, and its origins date back to the 15th century in Scotland.

Types of Golf Clubs

There are several types of golf clubs, each designed for specific purposes:

  1. Woods
    • Driver: Used for long-distance tee shots.
    • Fairway Woods: Ideal for long shots from the fairway.
  2. Irons
    • Long Irons: For long-distance shots.
    • Short Irons: For precision shots around the green.
  3. Wedges: Used for short, high shots around the green.
  4. Putters: Essential for rolling the ball into the hole on the green.

Golf Tips for Beginners

Here are some essential tips to improve your golf game:

  • Nail the basic fundamentals of grip and stance.
  • Know how far your clubs go to improve consistency.
  • Practice short game shots like chips and putts.
